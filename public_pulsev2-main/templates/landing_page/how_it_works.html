How Public Pulse Works

Step 1: Submit Your Complaint

Users can easily submit complaints through our user-friendly interface. Simply describe your issue, add relevant details, and submit.

Step 2: AI-Powered Analysis

Our advanced AI system analyzes your complaint to categorize it, assess sentiment, and identify key issues automatically.

Step 3: Smart Categorization

Complaints are automatically categorized into relevant departments (Infrastructure, Healthcare, Education, etc.) for efficient processing.

Step 4: Priority Assignment

Based on urgency and impact, complaints are assigned priority levels to ensure critical issues are addressed first.

Step 5: Government Action Tracking

Track the progress of your complaint in real-time. Get notifications when there are updates or resolutions.

Step 6: Feedback & Improvement

After resolution, provide feedback to help us improve the system. Your input helps enhance public services.
