How do I lodge a complaint?

Go to "Lodge Complaint" page, fill in your details, select the category, describe your issue in detail, and submit. You will receive a unique Complaint ID for tracking.

How do I track my complaint?

Use the "Track Complaint" page and enter your Complaint ID. You can also login to your account to see all your complaints and their current status.

What is a Complaint ID?

A unique identification number generated when you submit a complaint. It starts with "PP" followed by date and random characters (e.g., PP20231215ABC12). Keep this ID safe for tracking your complaint.

How long does it take to resolve complaints?

Resolution time varies: Simple issues: 3-5 working days, Moderate issues: 5-10 working days, Complex issues: 10-15 working days. You will receive regular updates about your complaint's progress.

What file types can I upload with my complaint?

You can upload images (JPG, PNG, GIF), PDF documents, and Word files (DOC, DOCX). Maximum file size is 10MB per file, with up to 5 files per complaint. Supported formats help us better understand and process your complaint.

Is registration free?

Yes! Public Pulse is completely free for citizens. There are no registration fees, subscription charges, or hidden costs. Our service is funded to promote transparent governance.

What if I forget my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Do I need to create an account to lodge a complaint?

You can submit complaints without an account, but creating one helps track all complaints easily and provides additional features like notifications and complaint history.

How does the AI chatbot work?

Our AI chatbot uses natural language processing to understand your queries. It can help you draft complaints, track existing complaints, answer questions about the process, and guide you through the system. It's available 24/7 for instant assistance.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our AI system has an accuracy rate of over 92% for complaint categorization and priority detection. It continuously learns from new data and improves over time. All AI suggestions are reviewed by our system administrators before final processing.

How does AI help in complaint resolution?

AI analyzes complaints to automatically categorize them, determine priority levels, detect sentiment for urgency, extract keywords, and route them to the appropriate departments. This reduces processing time by up to 80% compared to manual methods.

Is my personal data safe?

Absolutely! We use end-to-end encryption, secure socket layer (SSL) technology, and comply with all data protection regulations. Your personal information is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can complaints be submitted anonymously?

Yes, you can submit complaints anonymously. However, anonymous complaints may have lower priority as we cannot provide updates or request additional information. For the best experience and resolution, we recommend using a registered account.

How is my complaint data protected?

All complaint data is stored on secure servers with multiple layers of encryption. Access is strictly controlled and logged. We regularly audit our security measures and comply with international data protection standards.

What are your customer support hours?

Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (local time). For urgent issues, you can use our AI chatbot which is available 24/7. You can also email us at support@publicpulse.com for non-urgent queries.

How can I contact your support team?

You can contact us via email at support@publicpulse.com, use the contact form on our website, or call our helpline at +254 112 516 719 during business hours. For the fastest response, use our AI chatbot available 24/7.
